The Order
Process:

1)Scan

When a restaurant partners with us we provide them with a tablet and QR codes for each of their tables.

2)Order

The customer selects their food and then pays. Once the payment goes through, the order will be created.

3)Pay

The order will pop up on the tablet and show which table the order came from.

THE BENEFITS OF SWIFTIE

Faster table turnover.

Reducing the amount of time it takes to order and pay for the food allows the restaurant to cater to more guests than before.

Removes language barriers.

The restaurant's menu will be translated into the same language that the user's phone is set to.

Group friendly.

Everyone in a group can order and pay for their food individually. Swiftie removes the hassle of having to split a group's check.

Call a waiter from the table.

Users can also call a waiter to their table from the QR code.

Analytics and taxes.

Start collecting and analyzing data with Swiftie. Understand which items on your menu are most popular and when. Swiftie also keeps track of your costs of goods sold, revenue, and profit. So when it's time to file taxes you have all the information you need.

More busy, less
chaotic.

Have a busy restaurant? Let Swiftie take orders and accept payments. Customers won't have to wait on a waiter anymore!
